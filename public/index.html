About our school

Empowering students from humble and rural upbringings for success in the global careers worldwide.

Our school embodies the values of secularity, social conscience, discipline, perseverance and continued effort, which form the foundation of our educational philosophy.

Established in the year 1984, we believe and cater quality education for everyone irrespective of their differences.

Highlights

  • In the academic year 2014 - 2015, our student Jeyaraman secured third rank in State Level tenth public examinations.
  • Hakini Hall, a knowledge hub, employs contemporary visual aids to facilitate vibrant and engaging learning experiences.
  • We give sheer importance to computer applications skills. We offer comprehensive instruction in C, C++ and Programming to the students.
  • Thriving teaching communities foster transformative learning environments for remarkable student outcomes.
  • Heritage blooms under the nurture of values-infused education.
  • Serene embrace of nature’s tranquility, surrounded by lush greenery, creativity and imagination flourish with effortless grace.
  • School buses prioritize student safety with advanced monitoring systems.

Academic Excellence

Our school caters to students spanning from Kindergarten to Secondary Education, providing a thorough and enriching education that empowers them to seize opportunities beyond the confines of our campus.

Infrastructure

Good and serene campus with occupancy upto 1100 students. It is located just 300m east of Eral Bus Stand.

Why choose us?

Committed to deliver a high-value education, we prepare students for a successful future. Our focus on academics is complemented by a diverse range of extracurricular activities, sports, martial arts and leadership programs to foster talent and skill development. Furthermore, we recognize the contemporary importance of communication, computer applications and language development, providing students with the skills needed for today’s challenges.

Self Development

We are dedicated to foster the holistic development of our students, including their
self-development. We believe in equipping students with essential skills and qualities that will contribute to their personal growth and future success. We aim to empower our students with the necessary skills and qualities to navigate challenges and thrive in an ever-changing world.

Spirituality

We believe in instilling a strong sense of spirituality and guiding students to develop core values that will serve as a compass throughout their lives.

Through various activities, guest speakers and community service projects, we encourage students to develop a sense of purpose and cultivate values that will guide their actions and interactions with others.

Community

We place great importance on community engagement and hard work. As our school motto “Labor omnia vincit” which means, “Work conquers all!”

By nurturing a community-driven mindset and emphasizing the value of hard work, we empower our students to become responsible citizens who make a difference in their society.
